SESAS - Sustainable Energy Storage Application System

An Advanced Multi-Tasking Tool with Energy Protocol Management

Field of the Invention

The SESAS is designed for tightening and loosening nuts and bolts while managing energy protocols and providing power backup. It operates under AC, DC, and no power conditions.

Background

Traditional tools require manual effort or a dedicated power source. SESAS overcomes these limitations by ensuring operational flexibility regardless of power conditions.

Summary of the Invention

SESAS integrates a microcontroller-based energy management system, an energy storage facility, and embedded tools to enhance functionality.
